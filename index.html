BOOKS

The Nail Gunner

A string of gruesome murders of people impaled with nails shakes the very foundations of sanity. Detective Joseph Sharpe has seen his fair share of horrors but has yet to encounter his biggest fear. The heinous murder of a girl in a closed hospital room raised terror in the local people. Detective Joseph Sharpe is assigned the case to investigate the nail gun killings and digs into the depth of the crime happening with students of Willbrook High. As Detective Sharpe dives headfirst into this living nightmare, he discovers that the truth lurking beneath the case is as sinister as the murders themselves. Each step of his investigation peels back the layers of a sinister conspiracy that threatens to consume everything he holds dear. With time running out, Detective Holden must navigate a labyrinth of lies and horrors while unraveling if he is dealing with an unholy demon. "The Nail Gunner: Unearthing the Unholy" is a heart-pounding horror thriller that will unweave the threads of the unholy world of lies buried with the dead..
